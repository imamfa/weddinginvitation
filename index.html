Our Journey

Sindy and Albert first met in college at Texas State University in 2014. Sindy first caught Albert’s eye in the Student Union during freshman orientation. He was too nervous to approach her at first, but later that day, mustered up some courage and said: “Hi-dee!”—mixing up “hi” and “howdy”. Fortunately, Sindy thought this was adorable and the rest is history.  

During college, Sindy and Albert traveled extensively—backpacking through Europe during their semester in Italy. One of their favorite memories is trying a new gelato place almost every day while visiting Rome. 

Albert proposed to Sindy a few months after college graduation by renting out a movie theater. Sindy thought she was seeing a screening of her favorite film, The Lion King , but Albert had created a slideshow of photos from throughout their relationship which was playing on the movie screen. Needless to say, Sindy said yes (and they did end up watching The Lion King !). 

We can’t wait to celebrate with you at our wedding ceremony and reception in December! 

Sindy & Albert
